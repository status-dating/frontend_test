    About

    We have created a top dating and networking community for Kansas City. Our company was built to provide a unique and fun way to connect singles in a city that doesn't have a great dating scene and to provide a way to connect them with people they normally wouldn't be able to meet on their own. We screen every applicant before they are a member with our special application and background check. Then they are vetted by a committee made up of diverse people around Kansas City with different professions and community backgrounds. Once someone becomes a member we use our special algorithm to match them with people they would like to meet. Everyone will be on our waiting list until they are approved. Our waiting list will also help us keep the ratio even between men and women in our system so there is no over saturation of either gender. If you make the waiting list don't worry, that means we are processing your application and we are working as fast as we can to screen all of our applicants. Most applications take anywhere from a few days to up to a month. We will notify you when your application has been approved. All application, background check, and membership information is private and confidential.

    Membership

    Join other singles in Kansas City and become a member of Status Dating! Status Dating memberships are for singles that are serious about dating and relationships. All STATUS members are local to the Kansas City area. They go through a screening process that includes a background check to ensure there are no fake profiles, and no shady backgrounds. We use a local and personal approach to matching singles.

    How it works

    It’s simple! To become a member just click sign up here on our website. Choose a membership package that works for you, then go through a quick screening process. You will be notified when your status is approved. If your status is not approved you only pay for the screening process and you will be refunded the cost of the membership package you chose.

    Criteria

    Memberships are only for those that meet STATUS requirements and pass our special screening process. We look for singles that can pass a background check and have an average or better professional status, and/or have an active social status in Kansas City.

    Membership Benefits

    Members attend our singles events for free, have access to use our consulting services, and are able to use our private database to search and connect with other members!
