Who We Are

A Dating Service Kansas City Needs!

Don’t expect a typical dating service where anyone can be a member. With most on-line dating services they care more about quanity than quality! With STATUS Dating you don’t have to waste your time with fake profiles or people you are afraid to meet in person. What you can expect is a company that provides real people, real dates, and real relationships! We are a selective dating service for the Kansas City area only. We connect quality members with other quality members through our events, consulting services, and website database. We use our special screening process to make sure each member is properly vetted with a background check and proper status to keep members local and save you time dealing with people who are on-line dating for all the wrong reasons. What do we mean by status? Well, to sum it up, after you pass a background check (see terms & conditions) we basically check your professional status and social status before you can be a member. Can you pass a background check? Do you have a job? Do you make a decent income? Do you have social media and is it real? What is your education level? We take all of these factors into consideration as well as other screening methods to determine our members. We came up with this process for a safer way to date and for quality members that we know are serious about finding a match. Not everyone will be eligible to be a member and not every one that is eligible may want to become a member. Don’t worry, if you aren’t able to become a member we will keep you on a waiting list and if your status changes you can let us know and you can reapply.
